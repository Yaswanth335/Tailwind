Description

Charitee is a modern Bootstrap 5 charity website template for your organization’s online
presence. This site canvas rocks a beautiful design for everyone to immerse into your content
much easier. You are welcome to work with Charitee out of the box, but you can also go
entirely AGAINST THE GRAIN.

The responsive structure smoothly adapts to smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Go ahead
and test it out by heading over to the live preview.

Call-to-action buttons, floating navbar, back-to-top button and integrated Google Maps are
some of the Charitee’s features. You can also share all your programs, campaigns and start
raising funds straight from your website. To make it all work properly, you must code and use
back-end to go to the next level with Charitee.
